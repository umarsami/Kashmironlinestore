Popular Destinations by SUV

Top places to explore in Kashmir with our SUV rental

Gulmarg - Meadow of Flowers

Just 51 km from Srinagar, Gulmarg is Asia's top skiing destination. The winding road to Gulmarg through pine forests is best experienced in an SUV. Enjoy the Gondola ride, snow activities, and stunning views of Nanga Parbat.

Pahalgam - Valley of Shepherds

Located 95 km from Srinagar, Pahalgam offers Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari. The roads require a sturdy vehicle, making SUVs the preferred choice. Also the base camp for Amarnath Yatra.

Sonmarg - Meadow of Gold

At 80 km from Srinagar on the Srinagar-Leh highway, Sonmarg offers Thajiwas Glacier and breathtaking alpine scenery. An SUV handles the highway and off-road glacier approaches with ease.
