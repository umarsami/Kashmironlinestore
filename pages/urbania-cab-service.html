What is Force Urbania?

India's most premium luxury van for group travel

The Ultimate Group Travel Vehicle

The Force Urbania is India's answer to luxury group travel. Built by Force Motors, it's a monocoque-body van that combines the comfort of a luxury car with the capacity of a mini-bus. Available in 12-seater and 17-seater configurations, it's the perfect vehicle for exploring Kashmir with your entire group.


Unlike traditional Tempo Travellers, the Urbania offers a bus-like body with flat floor, wide aisle, large windows, and a smooth ride quality that makes long mountain journeys truly enjoyable.

Urbania Key Features

  • 12-seater and 17-seater configurations available
  • Full AC with roof-mounted blower system
  • Pushback reclining seats with armrests
  • Flat floor design for easy movement inside
  • Large panoramic windows for scenic views
  • Ample luggage space for group baggage
  • USB charging ports at every seat
  • LED interior lighting and reading lamps
  • Powerful diesel engine for mountain roads
  • Advanced safety features - ABS, EBD, dual airbags
