Popular SUV Routes in Kashmir

Our SUVs cover all major tourist destinations and off-beat locations in Kashmir.

🏔

Srinagar to Gulmarg

51 km — Travel through pine forests to Asia's premier ski resort. SUV recommended for steep climb, especially in winters.

🏔

Srinagar to Pahalgam

95 km — The valley of shepherds. Enjoy scenic Lidder river views. Essential for Amarnath Yatra base camp access.

🏔

Srinagar to Sonmarg

80 km — Gateway to Ladakh. Stunning glaciers and meadows. 4x4 SUV essential beyond Sonmarg towards Zoji La.

🏔

Srinagar to Doodhpathri

42 km — The valley of milk. A hidden gem with lush green meadows. SUV strongly recommended for the last stretch.
